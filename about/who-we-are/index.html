Who’s Behind Synedrus

Michail Konstantinidis is a technologist with nearly 20 years of software development experience across London and Sydney, working across industries like finance, SaaS, property management, entertainment, and automotive. Over time, it became clear that delivering meaningful outcomes required more than just writing code — it required stepping beyond narrow delivery roles to engage with architecture, security, and strategy at a systems level.

As a technical lead, Michail has guided teams through mission-critical projects, balancing technical excellence with real-world delivery demands under tight timelines and evolving requirements. This experience shaped Synedrus’s core approach: deliver sharp, expert-led solutions that cut through complexity and focus on what truly moves projects forward.

Synedrus was built to be the kind of consultancy its founder always wanted to work in: focused, expert-led, free from layers that hinder cadence, and dedicated to delivering sharp, actionable solutions that drive real progress.

With deep, hands-on experience inside high-pressure delivery environments and a broad knowledge of cloud architecture and DevSecOps practices, Synedrus helps companies facing critical challenges: scaling systems, modernising legacy architectures, or embedding security into fast-moving pipelines.

Every engagement is shaped by senior-level expertise, delivering clear roadmaps, validated prototypes, and the strategic clarity ambitious companies need to move forward with confidence.
