How We Work

A clear, structured engagement model designed to deliver speed, clarity, and real architectural impact without the overhead

Synedrus delivers sharp, strategic consulting - not bloated delivery teams or endless implementation cycles. Every engagement is lean, focused, and structured to give you architectural clarity, validated direction, and the ability to move fast with confidence.

Here’s what working with us looks like:

1. Discovery

We start with a quick email exchange to understand your goals, pain points, and where you’re stuck. If there’s alignment, we book a free 30-minute discovery call to explore the problem in more depth and outline possible paths forward. No obligation.

2. Engagement Structure

We have two core engagement models: project-based delivery and ongoing advisory. Both are lean, focused, and built to give you clarity, not overhead.

Project Work

For high-stakes builds or transformations, we operate as a short-term strategic partner.

Each engagement follows a structured path:

  1. In-Depth Discovery – If needed, we dig deeper into your systems, constraints, and objectives to fully understand the context.

  2. System Design – We define the proposed architecture, laying down the structure, integration points, and key decisions.

  3. Rapid Prototyping – We validate core components early, surfacing risks and giving your team tangible direction.

  4. Handover – We package the validated design for your internal team (or chosen delivery partner) to implement - cleanly, with full documentation and support.

We stay engaged during implementation if needed, providing clarification, adjustments, or oversight to keep delivery on track - without stepping into full execution.

You retain control. We provide the clarity and velocity to get moving in the right direction, fast.

Advisory Retainers

For ongoing needs, we offer fractional architecture and strategic oversight - without the cost or commitment of a full-time hire.

Typical advisory work includes:

  • Architecture reviews
  • Technical sounding board for engineering leads
  • Oversight during critical phases
  • Hands-on input during design sprints or planning cycles

These retainers are flexible, scoped clearly, and designed to keep you moving without unnecessary drag.

You know what’s being delivered, when, and what success looks like.
