Disaster Readiness and Recovery

Find the holes in your incident response before a real incident does.

Get in touch

Don’t wait for a crisis to find out you weren’t ready.

A small mistake, like an expired login, a broken update, or the wrong setting, can quietly take down part of your system. The real question is: would anyone know? Would the right warnings go off? Would the system bounce back, or would everything grind to a halt?

This service safely simulates a real-world failure in your staging environment and shows you what your systems actually do when something goes wrong. No theory, but hard evidence of what works and what doesn’t.

We then give you a focused list of fixes to close the gaps so when something breaks in production, it doesn’t become an emergency.

What’s Included:

  • A realistic failure simulation in a non-production environment
  • Observation of system behaviour: detection, alerting, fallback, or failure
  • A punch list of weaknesses and missing controls
  • Clear recommendations for improvement
  • Optional support to implement the highest-priority fixes

Ideal For:

  • Businesses with complex systems and no recent failure testing
  • Businesses with new infrastructure, new team members, or upcoming go-lives
  • Companies under pressure to prove operational maturity before audits or enterprise deals

We’ll show you where the cracks are before they matter.
