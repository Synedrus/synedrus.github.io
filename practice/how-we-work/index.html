How We Work

Synedrus is built for time-sensitive technical problems without the overhead

When something is broken, risky, or blocking delivery, you don’t need a strategy roadmap. You need it fixed.

Synedrus engagements are short, structured, and laser-focused on solving a specific technical problem.

What to Expect

Each engagement is structured as a fixed-scope sprint. No lock-in. No bloated process. Just the right structure to keep things clean and moving.

1. Discovery

We start with a quick email exchange to understand your goals, pain points, and where you’re stuck. If there’s alignment, we book a free 30-minute discovery call to explore the problem in more depth and outline possible paths forward. No obligation.

2. Engagement Structure

We tailor the sprint to the problem you’re facing—whether that’s security, compliance, delivery, or disaster simulation.

1. In-Depth Discovery

If needed, we dig deeper into your systems, constraints, and objectives to fully understand the context.

2. System Design (If Applicable)

For architecture-heavy work (e.g. stalled migrations, unstable pipelines), we sketch the target design: structure, key components, integration points, and critical trade-offs. This avoids wasted effort and sets the direction.

If the job is tactical (e.g. security hardening or audit remediation), we skip this and get straight to execution.

3. Execution

Whether it’s rewriting IAM policies, refactoring brittle services, or implementing the fixes your compliance officer flagged, we roll up sleeves and get it done. Senior-level, and with zero ceremony.

4. Handover

When the work’s done, you’ll get a clear handover: what changed, what risk was mitigated, and what to monitor going forward. If short-term support is needed during rollout, that can be arranged, but only if it adds value.


You get a fixed-fee engagement, scoped tightly around the problem that matters right now. The focus is solving the immediate issue—cleanly, and with full accountability.
